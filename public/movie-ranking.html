2.Manjumel Boys

"Manjummel Boys" takes audiences on an exhilarating journey as it explores the resilience of the human spirit when faced with adversity. Anchored by a gripping storyline centered around a man's harrowing experience of falling into a cave, the film expertly blends suspense, drama, and moments of sheer determination. Through breathtaking cinematography and compelling performances, viewers are drawn into the protagonist's struggle for survival and the unwavering support of his friends. "Manjummel Boys" is a riveting tale of courage, friendship, and the triumph of the human will, leaving audiences on the edge of their seats until the very end.
